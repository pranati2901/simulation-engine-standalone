SimCore — Data Connectors

What we connect to, and what we need from your side

NextXR · SimCore
How it works. SimCore never invents results. It builds a live model (a semantic asset graph) of your real operation from the systems below, then the simulation engine computes outcomes on that model. The more you connect, the sharper the twin — but a pilot starts with a small MINIMUM set and expands to the FULL picture over time. All access is read-only; no control commands are ever sent to your equipment.

Formats, pilot scope & security

  • Formats: read-only API, OCPP feed, SQL / time-series DB, MQTT/Kafka, or CSV/Excel export. We are hardware-agnostic and speak the standards — no changes to your stack.
  • Pilot minimum: the MIN rows for a single site (3–5 stations). Everything else sharpens accuracy and can be added incrementally.
  • Security: read-only; data can be anonymised (asset codes, not addresses); no control signals are ever issued to your equipment.
